Business Questions:
- What types of products does the company sell, and where are customers located?
- Are there any seasonal patterns or trends for order volume or revenue?
- How long is the average delivery time in days? Has that changed over time?

Tools: Power BI, Microsoft Excel.

Business Answers:
- The category of products sold by the company are:
    - Audio
    - Cameras and Camcorders
    - Cell Phones
    - Computers
    - Games and Toys
    - Home Appliances
    - Music, Movies, and Audio Books
    - TV and Video
 - The customers are located in:
   - United States
   - United Kingdom
   - Canada
   - Germany
   - Australia
   - Netherlands
   - France
   - Italy
- The order volume had the same seasonal pattern and trend as the revenue, with orders and revenue peaking every December and February.
- The average delivery time is 5 days. From 2016 to 2021, there has been a significant improvement in the average delivery time.
